About Me

As an AI Engineer and Cognitive Technologist based in Karlsruhe, Germany, Michael Lorenz holds a Master's equivalent degree (Dipl.-Ing./M.Sc.) in Energy & Environment Technology from KIT. He specializes in leveraging AI for complex system challenges. His focus areas include AI-powered Digital Twins, advanced Battery Analytics, scalable Cloud Infrastructure design, and the analysis of Cognitive Systems. He is passionate about driving sustainable system innovation through data-driven insights and strategic technological application, combining analytical rigor with creative, multiperspective problem-solving.

A graduate of the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), he engages critically with technological trends and their societal implications. His goal is to develop and apply technology in a way that is not only efficient but also responsible and human-centered.
